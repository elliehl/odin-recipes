Amerikaner

Description

This light sponge cake is a common German snack in many bakeries.

The name 'Amerikaner' literally translates to 'American'.

Ingredients

  • 120g Butter
  • 100g Sugar
  • 0.5 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 2 Eggs
  • 45g Flour
  • 2 tsp Baking Powder
  • 100g Powdered Sugar
  • 3 tbsp Water

Steps

  1. Crack and whisk the eggs
  2. Add flour, baking powder, sugar and vanilla extract to the egg mixture, then whisk
  3. Line a baking tray with baking paper, then spoon out circles of mixture onto it
  4. Bake the mixture at 180 degrees celsius for 20 minutes
  5. Mix powdered sugar with water, then spread onto flat side of newly baked Amerikaner
  6. Cut into any shape preferred and add toppings if you'd like!
