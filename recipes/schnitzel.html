Schnitzel

Description

Schnitzel is a dish made up of one of a number of meats (such as chicken, turkey, pork, or more), then covered with breadcrumbs.

There are many variations of the recipe, and this page will focus on the German pork version.

Ingredients

  • 300g Pork Steak
  • 2 Eggs
  • 100g Breadcrumbs
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • Salt and Pepper Seasoning

Steps

  1. Season the pork steak with salt and pepper on both sides
  2. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk them
  3. Pour the breadcrumbs onto a plate
  4. Place the pork steak into the breadcrumbs and rotate
  5. Dip the pork steak into the egg mixture
  6. Dip this egg covered pork steak into the breadcrumbs and rotate until evenly covered
  7. Repeat this process with all other pieces of pork
  8. Put the olive oil into a frying pan and heat it
  9. Place the pork steaks into the frying pan
  10. Turn over the pork steaks once they have browned on one side
  11. Finally, to ensure they are properly cooked, place the now finished schnitzel into the oven at 150 degrees celsius for 10 minutes
