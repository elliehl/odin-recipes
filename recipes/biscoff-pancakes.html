Biscoff Pancakes

Description

Pancakes are quick and simple to make, but they're always delicious!

This recipe adds biscoff as a filling for the extra sweet flavour!

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 125ml Milk
  • 150g Flour
  • 3 tbsp Biscoff
  • 1 tsp Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Crack open the eggs and whisk them
  2. Add the milk to the eggs and mix
  3. Add flour in regular intervals while whisking into a smooth batter of pancake mixture
  4. Heat frying pan and add olive oil
  5. When oil is hot, add one ladle of pancake mixture
  6. Wait until pancake mixture browns then flip over
  7. Brown the other side to the same level
  8. Add biscoff and you're finished!
